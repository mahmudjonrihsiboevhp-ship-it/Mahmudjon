Made to be worked in, not just worn.

FIELDWORK started in a two-person sewing room behind a hardware store, patching work jackets that ripped faster than they should. Twelve years later we still cut every pattern for movement first — flat-felled seams, gusseted shoulders, bar-tacked stress points — and test each style on the people who'll actually wear it out.
