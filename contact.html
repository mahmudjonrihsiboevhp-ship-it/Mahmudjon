About FIELDWORK

A workshop first, a shop second.

Everything we make still gets cut, sewn and tested three blocks from where we started.

How it started

FIELDWORK opened in 2014 as a one-room repair counter behind a hardware store, patching work jackets that ripped faster than the job allowed. Customers started asking us to build the jacket right the first time — so we did. Twelve years on, the same principle runs the whole company: cut for movement, reinforce the stress points, and stand behind every seam.

We still cut and sew everything in-house, in small batches, and every garment ships with a repair card. Wear it out, send it back, we'll fix it.

Before you ask

Frequently asked

How does the 40-year stitch warranty work?

If a seam fails from normal wear, send the garment back with your order number. We repair it free of charge; you only cover return shipping.

What's your sizing like?

Outerwear and workwear run true to size with room for a mid-layer. Full measurements are listed on every product page — when between sizes, we recommend sizing up.

How long does shipping take?

Orders ship within two business days. Standard delivery takes 3–6 business days; expedited options are available at checkout.

Do you accept returns?

Unworn items in original condition can be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Repairs follow the stitch warranty terms above instead.
